## 1. What is IPE City?

Ipe City (stylized "Ipe") is a long-term initiative to build social technologies for a new, AI- and crypto-native urban center. It positions itself as a **network society** of techno-optimists working to prototype governance systems and parallel institutions using digital and decentralized technologies.

Ipe Village is the first major prototype phase -- a one-month pop-up city where hundreds of builders live together, ship products, and stress-test governance experiments in the real world. It is branded as "Brazil's largest buildathon."

The project addresses a fundamental governance gap: modern economies operate on digital-native systems (internet, blockchains, cryptography) while governance systems remain rooted in 250-year-old institutional frameworks designed for a world of high communication costs, high barriers to mobility, strong citizen ties to geography, and information scarcity -- conditions that no longer exist.

## 2. Mission and Core Values

### Mission

Demonstrate that startups can build new cities and governance systems by prototyping what an AI- and crypto-native city looks like in the real world.

### Core Values

- **Pro-technology**: Embracing AI, crypto, blockchain, and decentralized systems as tools for human progress
- **Pro-freedom**: Advocating for individual sovereignty, digital identity ownership, and freedom from legacy institutional constraints
- **Pro-human progress**: Addressing longstanding societal challenges such as chronic poverty, inefficient healthcare, and human rights violations through innovation
- **Techno-optimism**: Fostering a culture that believes technology can and should transform governance for the better
- **Open collaboration**: Sharing ideas, code, and content openly with the community

### Governance Philosophy

Rather than attempt to reform existing state monopolies (deemed impossible due to their monopolistic nature), the approach mimics tech startups: start small on a clean slate, innovate in some of the governance layers, and then scale up. The project endorses Network States and Startup Societies as revolutionary components for evolving the political, governance, and civic stack.

## 3. Three-Layer Strategy

Ipe City operates across three interconnected strategic layers:

### Social Layer

- Build a community of techno-optimistic builders, founders, creators, and visionaries
- Foster culture around freedom, progress, and fairness
- Create coliving and co-creation spaces where participants form teams, build products, and launch startups
- Community events including meetups, workshops, fitness activities, tourism, and social gatherings
- Open event calendars and coordination via Discord and Telegram

### Technological Layer

- Prototype and deploy real city infrastructure: digital identity, onchain governance, contribution tracking, public dashboards, and marketplaces
- Leverage digital and decentralized technologies (blockchain, AI, cryptography, zero-knowledge proofs)
- Build solutions for governance, education, coordination, identity, safety, healthcare, and other government-related services
- Themed Hacker Houses focused on specialized technology tracks

### Economic Layer

- Internal token economy ($IPE) to track and reward contributions
- Onchain reputation systems creating transparent, verifiable achievement records
- Grant programs and prize pools for builders
- Talent matching, investor access, and job opportunities
- Work becomes a live portfolio visible to partners, investors, and hiring teams
- Revenue from passport sales with tiered pricing

## 4. Governance Model

### Startup Societies Approach

Ipe City views governance as an industry ripe for disruption, just as technology startups transformed legacy industries. The model advocates for:

- **Governance competition**: Multiple startup societies competing to offer better governance, rather than monopolistic nation-states
- **Clean-slate innovation**: Building new governance architectures from scratch rather than modifying existing ones
- **Lean governments**: Startup societies that create leaner governments and include communities at the core of decision-making
- **Parallel institutions**: Building alternative systems for governance, finance, education, health, and public safety -- domains historically monopolized by traditional government

### The Grounding Engine

An AI system that reviews each governance proposal for risks and internal contradictions before the vote opens. This embodies "w/acc" (wisdom acceleration) -- AI sharpening rather than replacing collective judgment. First deployed at ZuKas Season 02 in Kas, Turkey, with Glen Weyl.

### AI-Driven Governance Experiments

- The Synthesis: First hackathon with AI agents as judges alongside humans. 685 projects competed for $100k+ in prizes, evaluated through an "agentic judging layer" via Bonfires.ai
- This pattern is scalable to grants, retroPGF, DAO proposals, and dispute resolution

### Startup Society Conference

Held during Village (April 10-11, 2026 at IL Campanario Villaggio Resort), featuring 18+ speakers across governance, startup cities, crypto, privacy, and decentralized AI decision-making tracks. Free entry for Explorer and Architect passport holders.

## 5. PULSE System

### Overview

PULSE is Ipe City's coordinated action mechanism, designed to replace the low-bandwidth, 4-year voting process of traditional governance with a more fluid, high-frequency coordination system.

### Timing and Frequency

- Occurs **twice monthly**, on the **2nd and 16th** of each month
- Each Pulse operates within a **24-hour window** for task completion
- Pulses are unveiled in the dedicated **Pulse channel on Discord**

### Task Structure

- Initial tasks involve liking and sharing posts on Twitter/X to amplify community reach
- Tasks evolve over time in complexity
- A basic Pulse option always remains accessible to all, taking only a few minutes

### Layered Participation

- Future plans include different layers of Pulses with different types of tasks and different levels of obligation
- A fundamental basic Pulse will always exist for broad participation
- More complex layers for deeper engagement and contribution

### Participation Requirements

- To submit content proposals for community voting, members must have completed **all of the last 10 Pulses**
- While not explicitly labeled "mandatory," the consecutive completion requirement creates a strong expectation of ongoing participation
- The basic layer implies baseline contribution expectations

### Mission Alignment

- All Pulses maintain strict alignment with the project's mission
- Members' exceptional work may be promoted via Pulse to the broader community
- Pulses help members grow their social media presence and reach larger audiences

## 6. Resident Tiers

### Ipe Netizen

- **Definition**: Base-level credential granted to all participants and community members
- **Requirements**: Join the community
- **Benefits**: Basic community access, ability to participate in Pulses, access to Discord and Telegram channels
- **Grant eligibility**: Every Ipe Netizen can apply for grants for projects and content

### Ipe Explorer

- **Definition**: Learning and networking focused tier for those who want to learn, connect, and participate while maintaining remote work
- **Requirements**: 5+ days engagement with projects (per original 2025 model); 1-4 week stays available for Village
- **Benefits**:
  - Full coworking access at Founder Haus
  - Daily breakfast
  - All community events
  - 6-month access to the online Ipe Community
  - Free entry to Startup Society Conference
  - Remote work compatible

### Ipe Architect

- **Definition**: High-commitment building tier for those ready to ship products
- **Requirements**:
  - Minimum **3-week residency** at the Village
  - Application required (limited to **80 spots**)
  - Weekly progress requirements on project
  - Final project presentation at Demo Day
- **Benefits**:
  - All Explorer benefits included
  - **Exclusive access to grants and prizes** ($10k+ pool)
  - Verifiable onchain reputation awards
  - **1-year access** to the Ipe Community (vs 6 months for Explorer)
  - Top performers unlock global opportunities
  - 5 scholarship spots available
  - One free month at Balaji's Network School in Singapore
  - Investor access and talent matching

### Team / Founder Passport

- **Definition**: Product launchpad tier for startups and protocols
- **Requirements**: Bring core team to the Village
- **Benefits**:
  - Treat the Village as a live testing ground
  - Daily iteration based on user feedback from Village residents
  - Integration into Village program for real-economy validation

## 8. Token System ($IPE)

### Purpose

The Village runs on its own internal economy using $IPE tokens to explicitly track and reward contributions.

### Key Properties

- **Contribution tracking**: Whether shipping code or supporting the community, impact is measured and rewarded
- **Transparent reputation**: Creates a transparent onchain reputation system
- **Verifiable achievements**: Onchain credentials, grants, tokens, and additional rewards for contributors
- **Public dashboards**: Contribution data visible on public dashboards

### 2025 Results (Proof of Concept)

- $3,600+ transacted onchain during the first Village edition
- Demonstrated viability of token-based internal economy
