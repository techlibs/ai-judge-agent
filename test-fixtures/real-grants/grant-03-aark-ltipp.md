# Aark — Perpetual DEX User Acquisition

**Grant Program**: Arbitrum Long Term Incentives Pilot Program (LTIPP), March 2024
**Amount**: 900,000 ARB requested + 8,000,000 AARK (~$1.6M) matching funds
**Source URL**: https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/aark-ltipp-application-final/21464

## Project Description

Aark is a perpetual DEX offering up to 1000x leverage with an "Intervals" liquidity structure that enables single-sided LPing using diverse collateral — LSTs, LRTs, and RWAs. The team claims this collateral profile is unique among perp DEXs. The LTIPP proposal funds a 12-week user-acquisition and liquidity-bootstrapping push on Arbitrum targeting cross-chain trader migration, new LP assets, and a referral flywheel.

## Team

14-person team led by CEO Eden, spanning smart-contract engineering, backend, and infrastructure. Pre-seed backers include Delphi Digital and OKX Ventures, plus additional seed-round firms. Aark is a relatively new entrant compared to incumbent Arbitrum perps (GMX, Vertex).

## Milestones & Deliverables

- **Week 4**: Onboard 1,050+ users (50% of 2,100 target), primarily cross-chain
- **Week 8**: List 3+ new LP collateral types (additional LSTs/LRTs/RWAs)
- **Week 12**: 20% growth in active wallets driven by referral network
- Weekly on-chain dashboards for incentive distribution
- Post-program report

## Budget Breakdown

900,000 ARB allocated:
- **270,000 ARB (30%)** — Acquisition of non-Arbitrum users via targeted airdrops
- **180,000 ARB (20%)** — LP incentives for new collateral types
- **270,000 ARB (30%)** — Trading incentives (volume-weighted)
- **180,000 ARB (20%)** — Referral rewards

Sized using a $250 customer-acquisition-cost assumption derived from an $800 LTV benchmark taken from comparable protocols like GMX.

## Impact Claims

- Acquire 2,100+ non-Arbitrum users, enlarging the chain's trader base
- Introduce novel collateral types (LRTs, RWAs) previously unavailable for perp margin on Arbitrum
- Drive organic wallet growth via a viral referral loop
- Matching $1.6M in native AARK tokens substantially amplifies ARB leverage

## Outcome (if known)

Approved after contentious forum debate. Critics surfaced a prior airdrop event where alleged server failures prevented eligible participants from claiming rewards, followed by Discord channel suppression. The team attributed issues to unexpected traffic and DDoS attacks and acknowledged "immature demand estimation." Supporters countered that issues were eventually resolved and praised team responsiveness and product innovation. A useful test case for a Team-Capability judge weighing reputation signals and a Cost-Efficiency judge evaluating CAC assumptions.
