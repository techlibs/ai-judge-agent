# Aave Protocol — GHO Growth on Arbitrum

**Grant Program**: Arbitrum Long Term Incentives Pilot Program (LTIPP), March 2024
**Amount**: 750,000 ARB (received)
**Source URL**: https://forum.arbitrum.foundation/t/aave-ltipp-application-final/21741

## Project Description

Aave is the leading liquidity protocol in Decentralized Finance and the second-largest project on Arbitrum by TVL. The protocol enables permissionless crypto lending and borrowing without traditional intermediaries. This LTIPP proposal funds a 12-week incentive program to (1) launch and grow cross-chain GHO, Aave's decentralized stablecoin, on Arbitrum and (2) increase user retention on Aave V3 Arbitrum by subsidizing borrow economics for WETH, stablecoins, and GHO.

## Team

Submitted by the Aave Chan Initiative (ACI) on behalf of the Aave DAO. ACI is led by Marc Zeller (Founder) with governance leads, developers, and designers. Aave DAO employs established service providers including Avara, BGD Labs, Chaos Labs, and Karpatkey. Aave is one of the most battle-tested protocols in DeFi with multi-year operational history and audits from top firms.

## Milestones & Deliverables

- **Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4)**: Target >25% GHO borrow-cap utilization on Arbitrum
- **Phase 2 (Weeks 5–8)**: Target >50% GHO supply utilization
- **Phase 3 (Weeks 9–12)**: Sustain >75% utilization and measurable TVL growth
- Bi-weekly community updates referencing OpenBlock Labs dashboards
- Final closeout report within two weeks of program conclusion
- Full compliance with LTIPP data-reporting requirements

## Budget Breakdown

- **375,000 ARB** — Borrow incentives for WETH, stablecoins, and GHO supplied/borrowed on Aave V3 Arbitrum
- **375,000 ARB** — GHO liquidity mining rewards managed by the GHO Liquidity Committee

Total: **750,000 ARB** distributed linearly over 12 weeks.

## Impact Claims

- Bootstraps cross-chain GHO on Arbitrum, diversifying stablecoin supply away from centralized issuers
- Improves borrow economics, making strategies on Arbitrum more profitable and attracting sticky TVL
- Drives retained users to Aave V3 Arbitrum; onboarding leverage comes from GHO's cost-effective medium-of-exchange profile
- Facilitates partnerships across the Arbitrum DeFi ecosystem (DEXs, money markets, yield aggregators) by deepening GHO liquidity

## Outcome (if known)

Approved by the LTIPP Council and executed. Aave GHO launched on Arbitrum in 2024; the incentive program ran over the 12-week window with bi-weekly OpenBlock reporting. Aave remains the #2 protocol on Arbitrum by TVL throughout and after the program.
